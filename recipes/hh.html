HamburgerHelper Recipe(Beef Noodle)

Description

HamburgerHelper is a pasta dish that is cooked with ground beef and the noodles that they provide in the package. In the package they provide the seasoning required to complete the dish and other then that all you need is a littel bit of milk and water along with your ground beef and it is an easy dinner cooked within 15min.

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, for 7 to 8 minutes, breaking into small chunks and stirring constantly until browned.
  2. Stir in Sauce Mix and hot water until dissolved.
  3. Stir in milk and Pasta.
  4. Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.
  5. Reduce heat, cover and simmer about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is tender.
